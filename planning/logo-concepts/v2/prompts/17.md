Production-grade horizontal lockup brand logo for "Humi" — an enterprise HR platform. Square 1:1 canvas, pure white background.

Composition: HORIZONTAL lockup — mark on the LEFT, wordmark "humi" on the RIGHT. Both vertically centered.

PRIMARY MARK (left side, ~32% of canvas width): Three overlapping solid-filled circles arranged in a tight triangular cluster — one circle on top-left, one on top-right, one below centered between them (forming a small triangle of circles). Each circle is filled with a semi-transparent brand color with soft multiply/overlap blending where they intersect, creating rich secondary colors at the overlaps:
  Top-left circle: warm teal (#14B8A6) at ~80% opacity
  Top-right circle: warm amber (#F59E0B) at ~80% opacity
  Bottom-center circle: soft plum-rose (#C084FC) at ~80% opacity
Overlap zones create darker blended colors (teal+amber = olive-bronze, teal+plum = dusky blue, amber+plum = terracotta). Clean vector flat style — NOT photorealistic, just semi-transparent flat fills.

SECONDARY WORDMARK (right side, ~38% of canvas width): The word "humi" in lowercase (four letters h, u, m, i) in bold geometric sans-serif in deep navy (#0F172A). Wordmark vertically aligned with the center of the circles cluster.

Style reference: Mastercard's overlapping circles, Adobe Creative Cloud rainbow, Venn-diagram iconography — three brand colors intersecting to show "coming together".

Composition details: cluster on left, ~8% gap, wordmark on right. Lockup spans ~78% of canvas. Pure white background. Wordmark spelled EXACTLY "humi". Absolutely NO red, NO crimson.
