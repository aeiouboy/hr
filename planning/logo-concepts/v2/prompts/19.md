Production-grade brand logo for "Humi" — an enterprise HR platform. Square 1:1 canvas, pure white background.

Composition: Single-line wordmark "Hum" followed by a small person pictogram where the letter "i" would be. NO period at the end.

WORDMARK "Hum" (three letters — capital H, lowercase u, lowercase m): bold geometric sans-serif (Inter Bold / Söhne Bold / Neue Haas Grotesk feel), solid deep navy (#0F172A), evenly tracked.

PERSON PICTOGRAM (replacing the letter "i"): positioned on the same baseline as the wordmark, at the right end, roughly the width of a lowercase-i stem:
- HEAD: a perfect teal (#14B8A6) circle, sized like a large i-tittle, floating slightly above the body with a small gap
- BODY: teal (#14B8A6) rounded-rectangle stem, matching the x-height-plus-descender run below the head — essentially the height where an "i" stem would go
- Same teal hue throughout, no gradient, no shading
- No arms, no legs — purely minimal abstract person where the head-circle doubles as the i-tittle and the stem doubles as the i-stem

Spelling check: the only letters rendered are H, u, m (that's THREE letters). The person pictogram replaces "i" entirely. NOT "Humi" with an actual "i" letter. NO "Hum." with a period. NO extra glyphs.

Style reference: Airbnb Bélo + Atlassian wordmark minimalism, classic "person-as-letter" identity pattern, HR brand hinting people-first.

Composition: wordmark + person centered horizontally, spans ~60% of canvas, vertically centered. Pure white background. Absolutely NO red, NO crimson.
