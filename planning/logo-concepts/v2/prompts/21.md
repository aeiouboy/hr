Production-grade brand logo for "Humi" — an enterprise HR platform. Square 1:1 canvas, pure white background.

Composition: Single-line wordmark "Hum" followed by a small gradient-filled person pictogram where the letter "i" would be. NO period at the end.

WORDMARK "Hum" (three letters — capital H, lowercase u, lowercase m): bold geometric sans-serif (Inter Bold / Söhne Bold feel), solid deep navy (#0F172A), evenly tracked.

PERSON PICTOGRAM (replacing "i"): positioned on the baseline at the right end of the wordmark, roughly the width of a lowercase-i stem:
- HEAD: a perfect circle filled with warm amber (#F59E0B), sized like a large i-tittle, floating above the body with a small gap
- BODY: a rounded-rectangle stem filled with a smooth vertical linear gradient — warm teal (#14B8A6) at the top transitioning to soft coral-peach (#FB923C) at the bottom; matches x-height-plus-descender height
- No arms, no legs — purely minimal abstract person with two-part color play: solid amber head + gradient body
- Clean flat vector, no photorealism, no shadows

Spelling check: only H, u, m rendered as letters (three letters). Gradient person replaces "i" entirely. NO actual "i" character. NO period.

Style reference: Stripe gradient accent + classic "person-as-letter" HR brand — adds warmth and modernity to a minimal wordmark.

Composition: wordmark + person centered horizontally, spans ~60% of canvas, vertically centered. Pure white background. Absolutely NO red, NO crimson.
