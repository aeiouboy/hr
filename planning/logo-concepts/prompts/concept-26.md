Production-grade product logo for a SaaS platform named "Humi" — designed in the style of real enterprise wordmarks like SAP SuccessFactors, Odoo, Workday, PeopleSoft, Vercel.

CRITICAL: This is a REAL PRODUCTION WORDMARK LOGO. Not an illustration. Not a pictorial symbol. Nothing decorative. Just typography. Readable at favicon size.

Subject: the wordmark "Humi" spelled exactly H-u-m-i where "H" is capital and "u" "m" "i" are lowercase (title case: CapitalH-lowercase-u-lowercase-m-lowercase-i). Single word, no space, four characters total. Set in a modern bold geometric sans-serif. The capital H anchors the wordmark and the dotted i provides a subtle rhythm point.

Typography: clean geometric sans-serif, weight 700-800, slightly tight letter-spacing, optical kerning, with a very subtle custom touch — the capital "H" could have a slightly shortened crossbar or the dot of the "i" could be a rounded square instead of a circle, but overall must read as solid confident type first.

Color palette: monochromatic — entire wordmark in a single deep enterprise color: dark navy (#1E3A8A) OR deep indigo (#312E81) OR corporate charcoal (#111827). Pick one and use it for the whole word. Absolutely NO red, NO crimson, NO two-tone — single color only for this concept.

Composition: wordmark centered horizontally and vertically, 20% whitespace margin all sides, wordmark fills 75% of canvas width. Pure white background. NO accent mark, NO dot, NO icon, NO illustrations — just the pure typography. Square 1:1 canvas, readable at 32px favicon.
