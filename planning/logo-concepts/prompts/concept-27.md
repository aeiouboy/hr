Production-grade lettermark logo for a SaaS platform — designed in the style of custom-type brand marks like Airbnb's Bélo, Slack's old hash, Uber's wordmark, Mailchimp's Freddie mark (but WITHOUT any illustration of creatures/hands/objects — pure letterform only).

CRITICAL: This is a brand mark / lettermark. Real usable logo. Not an illustration. Not a scene. Just two custom letters forming a ligature.

Subject: the lowercase letters "hu" combined into a single custom ligature where the right stem of the "h" descends straight down and curves into the left side of the bowl of "u" — so the two letters share one continuous stroke (the right stem of h = the left stem of u). The ascender of the h rises taller. Rounded terminals. The result should look like a confident custom brand mark that could work alone as an app icon.

Typography: custom geometric rounded sans-serif, weight 700, lowercase only, both letters sitting on the same baseline, ascender on h. The shared stem between h and u is the key visual — it shows "a warm connected unit" (hu = first two letters of human / humi, emphasizing people-first HR DNA).

Color palette: solid color mark in deep teal (#0F766E) OR deep indigo (#3730A3) OR warm charcoal-amber gradient terminals (#1F2937 fading to #F59E0B at the right terminal of h). Pick one palette. Monochromatic or max 2-color. Absolutely NO red, NO crimson.

Composition: "hu" ligature centered on canvas, filling 55-60% of canvas height, 20% whitespace margin. Pure white background. NO wordmark below, NO tagline, NO illustration — just the letterform. Square 1:1, readable at 32px favicon, looks handsome at 2048px hero.
